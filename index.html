MTG Price lookup


Aprox. Price: $-.

Have you ever wondered how much a particular Magic the Gathering card is? Just type the card's name into the search bar, and find out what it's worth. from a few cents, to thousands of dollars, MTG cards vary widely in price.

For some card examples, look up these cards:
Harmless Offering
Shivan Dragon
Terastadon
Scalding Tarn
and if you're into spooky asthetics; Surgical Extraction.

(Card images and prices are provided through the Scryfall api. Some cards may not have a price due to rarity, in such cases, assume the price is "very high.")
